HISTROY

balamani Mall was the first enclosed shopping mall in india when it opened in 2003. In September 2007, the mall finished its $30 million renovation. This added a 450-seat food court, an additional 38,000 sq ft (3,500 m2) of space with a new anchor store Gottschalks, and a 3-story parking garage. The mall was acquired in 2006 by Newman Brettin Properties. In 2007 it was purchased by JP Realty Inc. of Salt Lake City. Macy's opened at the mall in 2009 in the former Gottschalks space which closed early that year. In 2018, Macy's added a Backstage discount store in its location. In 2016, Blaze Pizza opened at the mall.
